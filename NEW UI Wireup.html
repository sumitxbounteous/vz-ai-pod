Section 2

Service Level Agreement

Service Availability & Credits

Verizon commits to maintaining high service availability standards for all wireless data services provided under this agreement.

Service Credit Policy: Service credits shall be issued for interruptions exceeding 24 consecutive hours. Service credits shall be issued for interruptions exceeding 4 consecutive hours.

Service credits will be calculated as a percentage of the monthly service fee, prorated based on the duration of the outage. Customers must report service interruptions within 48 hours to be eligible for credits.

Notification Requirements:

Verizon will provide advance notification of planned maintenance windows that may affect service availability. Emergency maintenance may be performed without prior notice when necessary to maintain network integrity.

Section 3

Data Protection & Privacy Compliance

Data Handling & Security

Both parties acknowledge the importance of protecting customer data and personal information in accordance with applicable data protection regulations.

Compliance Standards: Provider must comply with GDPR, CCPA, and equivalent data protection regulations. Provider shall implement industry-standard security measures to protect customer data.

All data transmitted through Verizon's network shall be encrypted using current industry-standard protocols. Verizon maintains SOC 2 Type II certification and undergoes regular third-party security audits.

Data Breach Notification:

In the event of a data breach affecting customer information, Verizon will notify Hyundai Motor America within 72 hours of discovery and provide detailed information about the scope and impact of the breach.

Section 4

Payment Terms & Billing

Payment Schedule & Terms

All service fees shall be invoiced monthly in accordance with the pricing schedule outlined in Exhibit A.

Payment Terms: Payment is due within Net 30 days from the invoice date. Payment is due within Net 45 days from the invoice date.

Late payments will accrue interest at a rate of 1.5% per month or the maximum rate permitted by law, whichever is less. Hyundai Motor America may dispute any invoice by providing written notice within 15 days of receipt.
